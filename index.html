Why Cultural Coaching

Language is just the beginning: Italian culture is a colorful mix of gestures, customs, longstanding traditions and even words may mean completely different things based on context. If you really want to dig in Italian culture, we can teach you how to understand and read all these signs, that remain invisible to the novice.
